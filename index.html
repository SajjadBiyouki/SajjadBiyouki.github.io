About me

I am a PhD student in Industrial and Systems Engineering, minor in Computer Science at The University of Tennessee, Knoxville. I also obtain my MSc in Statistics and Data Science from Haslam College of Business, The University of Tennessee. Prior to my PhD, I completed my MSc and BSc degrees in Industrial Engineering and Management Systems at Amirkabir University of Technology (Tehran Polytechnic), where I worked as Graduate Research Assistant in Computational Intelligence Lab under the supervision of Prof. MH Fazel Zarandi.     Research

      My primary interests lie at the intersection of Statistics, Machine Learning and Operations Research, specialized in Pattern Recognition and Deep Learning. During my academic studies, I developed several machine learning techniques applied to healthcare image segmentation, remote sensing images, data-driven healthcare systems, distributed systems, and supply chain management applications.
