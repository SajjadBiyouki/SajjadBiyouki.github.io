About

I am an Operations Research Ph.D student along with Computational Science minor in the Department of Industrial and Systems Engineering at the University of Tennessee, Knoxville. Prior to my Ph.D, I completed my Msc and B.Sc degrees in Industrial Engineering and Management Systems at Amirkabir University of Technology (Tehran Polytechnic), where I have done research projects in Computational Intelligence Lab under the supervision of Prof. MH Fazel Zarand.

My primary interests are in the area of Statistical Machine Learning, Pattern Recognition, and Data-driven Decision Making, which I had some academic experience as research and teaching assistant. During my academic studies, I developed several machine learning techniques applied to healthcare image segmentation, remote sensing images, data-driven healthcare systems, distributed systems, and supply chain management applications.
